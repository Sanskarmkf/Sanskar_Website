Children Welfare: Uplifting Lives

Date: February 18, 2023

On this day, we spent a joyful day at Sri Saibaba Old Age & Orphan Children’s Home in Bengaluru. We distributed snacks and created happy memories, ensuring every child felt loved. This visit reinforced our commitment to uplifting vulnerable communities. Our goal is to provide support and joy to children in need, and the smiles we received made it all worthwhile. This program emphasizes our dedication to fostering a sense of belonging and care within our community.
