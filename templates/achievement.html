Achievement

MK Foundation is an organization initiated by engineering students of Acharya College, founded by Madhav Kumar Choudhary from Bihar and co-founder Shubham Kokila from Uttarakhand, Nainital. The leadership's vision for MK Foundation is to engage more people to contribute to the welfare of society. The organization has participants from various states across India. Within MK Foundation, three vital entities are MKOnlineClasses, Data Science Club, and Sanskar. "Sanskar" is an important organization actively involved in helping impoverished children and individuals. This organization not only provides education but also assists in fundraising for medical emergencies. Its main objective is to guide the underprivileged towards prosperity and bring positive changes in society. Members of this organization contribute a minimum of ₹20 each month, and whenever an opportunity arises, they extend help to people, sharing joy and happiness.
